ABOUT

Hi, I'm Scott. I enjoy building websites that integrate seamless function, attractive design and clear purpose. My approach to web development is to analyze a problem, think of a goal outcome, then create the best possible solution - of course having fun along the way.

Learning foreign languages has always been a passion of mine, I love to travel and explore new places. I also spend a lot of time outdoors, whether it's hiking, camping, running or gardening.
